Stickerio

About

Columbus is a great city, and most of us miss going out and visiting places special to Columbus. But post-Covid vaccine -- there needs to be a way to incentivize promoting Columbus's roots, small businesses, and parks. Each location has a sticker unique to them where if you visit, can you obtain a sticker while also logging into the system and input your sticker you earned. There are stickers for certain restaurants, parks, events, etc. Each unique to Columbus. The website will also show what stickers you have earned, and links to Columbus' many places to visit. There is a community effort page to handout a goal of 25000 stickers, and special stats showing how many stickers each were given out. There is a map page to show locations where stickers can be obtained along with a history page showing why it is special to Columbus. There is also a fun canvas page where you can digitally make a sticker collage. In all, Columbus is a very fun place to visit, and it is up to the community to revitalize Columbus and share memories about it. Columbus Stickerio aims to do just that.
